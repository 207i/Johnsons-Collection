# Johnson Collections

Johnson Collections is a creative fashion project focused on original sweater design, visual storytelling, and brand experimentation.

## Vision
To build a distinct clothing identity through art-driven pieces, starting with sweaters and expanding into full collections.

## Focus Areas
- Original sweater design
- Graphic and textile experimentation
- Brand identity development
- Product drops and collections
- Digital presence and marketing

## Roles
- Artist / Designer: Creative direction, artwork, product vision
- Developer / Marketer: Brand building, digital systems, growth strategy

## Goals
- Develop unique, recognizable designs
- Launch initial sweater collection
- Build an online presence and audience
- Scale into a full fashion brand

## Status
Active development — early stage concept and build phase.
